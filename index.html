My Services!

At Your Service

Manicure

A manicure will clean and sahpe your nails and treat your cuticles, which is vital for keeping your nails healthy and strong. During a manicure your hands will be exfoliated to remove dead skin cells and to improve circulation and stimulate blood flow.

Pedicure

Our feet can get tired from carrying our weight all day, a pedicure is a basic foot care as well as cosmetic treatment. Pedicures can keep your nails trimmred, calluses controlled and skin moisturized. In addition, you will get a foot massage with your pedicure to relieve tension and stimulate circulation.

Shellac

a quick, effective gel manicure hybrid that results in smudge-proof, chip-proof, glossy nail colour that lasts weeks. Shellac applies as quickly and easily as nail polish and requires minimal preparation beforehand, making it one of the fastest manicure options available.

Artificial Nails

For nail extensions you can choose bio or UV gels. I can make your nails mimic real fingernails that looks natural, and is light, If you wish I can design them for an artistic look.

Massage

Most of us can agree that getting a massage feels awesome. The purpose of massage tehrapy is to target the source of the body's pain via eliminating tense muscles, increasing flexibility, and providng relaxation to the affected muscles as well as the body as a whole.

Eyelashes

With eyelashes being so on trend at the moment, failing to enhance them means you're missing out on a 'must have' in your beauty routine. Apart from making your lashes thicker, darker and fuller, which is every woman's dream, eyelash extension also saves you a lot of time and effort getting ready in the morning.

Facial Treatment

Having regular facial treatments has lasting benefits will help your skin remain moisturized, fresh, and healthy. Whether you're seeking a thorough cleanings, pore refinement or anti-aging benefits, ou can speak to me about your needs and we can work out a treatment plan to target your specific areas of concern.

Permanent Makeup

Microblading is one of the hottest trends in makeup for brows. Other than completely filling and outlining your brows with color, the good thing about microblading is that it allows for placement of the hair like strokes into your skin. The procedure use a manual blade and targets the three layers of the skin. The new brows can last anywhere between 1 to 4 years depending on the environment exposure it gets.

Waxing

As a means of hair removal, waxing enjoys the following benefits: Long-term Hair Removal. Waxing works from the root hairs. No cuts or skin damage, waxing is rarely itchy. Fast and convenient process.
