About Me

Hi, my name is Kim,

I am a beautician based in Toronto. I have 20 years of experience providing nails and beauty services to thousands of clients across the GTA. Beyond the basic manicure and pedicure services, I can also do gel nails, shellac, and artificial nails. In addition, I also provide facial treatment services, waxing, and permanent makeups. And if you need to sooth muscle tensions you can come to me for a relaxing massage session.

Feel free to contact me if you have any questions, I look forward to speak to you.
